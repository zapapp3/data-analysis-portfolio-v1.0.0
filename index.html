ABOUT ME

I am Adebimpe Adetomiwa, a data analyst by profession, passionate about uncovering insights from numbers, searching for patterns, extracting meaning from data and turning it into actionable insights, specializing in making sense of complex information, analyzing data to drive decision-making. I thrive on transforming raw data / numbers into valuable insights. Using visualization tools like tableau to visualize the analyzed data for comfortable view of the data. I also specialize in database managements, managing of vast amount of data that is not suitable for flat files and other formats. Creating ERD (Entity Relationship Diagram) and converting ERD into database schema. Join me in the realm of data analysis and let's explore the potential of analytics together.
